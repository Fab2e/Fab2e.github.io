About Us 关于我们

Fab2E 第二实验学校数字工坊社团

Fab2E（第二实验学校数字工坊社团）成立于2015年，是学校科技社团之一，是最受学生欢迎的社团之一。社团注重技术学科与其他学科的整合，并在社团活动课程中引入激光切割、 2D和3D设计工具、电子设计、微控制器编程等课程课程，并将之应用于工程设计开发中。社团坚持关注新技术的发展及应用，并将学到的技术应用于科技创新等系列活动中。Fab2E周一至周五对本校学生开放，周六周日对社会开放。

Fab2E (Fab Lab of Second Experimental School) was founded in 2015, are one of the school science and Technology Association and the most popular student associations. Focus on integration of technology, society and other disciplines, we teach laser cutting, 2D and 3D design tools, electronic design, microcontroller programming course in community activities in the curriculum, and applied in engineering design. The community focuses on the development and application of new technology and applies this technology to the series of activities such as science and technology innovation.Fab2E is open to students from Monday to Friday and opens to the public on Saturday and Sunday.

深圳第二实验学校与Fab2E

第二实验学校是深圳市科技特色学校，每年获得政府在科技教育的资助超过500万，与深圳市本地的科技公司及大学院校有深度的合作。是深圳市STEM学会的发起单位，一直探索培养学生科技创新的活动及课程。2017年学校将一楼的2个实验室及会议室重新装修，用于FABLAB的建设，实验室面积近500平方，包括3D设计区，激光切割区，cnc加工区，电子编程设计区、小组讨论区等功能区，设备齐全，环境优雅，是学生最喜欢的实验室。　　在学校领导的大力支持下，Fab2e数字工坊社团的近年来在国内外青少年科技创新活动中等方面获得多个奖项

  1. 获2017深圳市优秀学生社团，在青少年科技创新大赛中多次获奖2.
  2. 2017First科技挑战赛（FTC）全国总决赛中获得全国总冠军（联盟队长）；
  3. 参加由许勤市长主持的深圳青年科学家与青少年座谈会；
  4. 应邀参加许勤市长机器人教育专题调研；
  5. 社团成员应邀参加卡耐基梅隆大学机器人学院暑假15天专题课程；
  6. 社团代表参加深圳大学数控机床CNC课程（2天）；
  7. 参与深圳市机器人教育实验室（2015-2020）规划工作；

Shenzhen Second Experimental School is a science and technology school in Shenzhen. It receives more than 5 million funding for science and technology education from the government every year and has deep cooperation with local science and technology companies and universities in Shenzhen. It is the initiating unit of Shenzhen STEM society and has been exploring the activities and courses of cultivating students’ scientific and technological innovation. In 2017, a laboratory and conference room are renovated for the construction of FABLAB laboratory, the area of nearly 500 square feet, including 3D design, laser cutting area, CNC area, electronic programming area, group discussion area and other functional areas, facilities, elegant environment, is the most popular laboratory of student.In support of the leadership of the school, we won several awards at home and abroad in recent years on youth science and technology innovation activities etc.:

  1. 2017 Shenzhen Excellent Student Community, and won many awards in the youth science and technology innovation competition;
  2. 2017 First (FTC), championship of China (league captain);
  3. Participate in the forum of Shenzhen young scientists and teenagers chaired by mayor Xu Qin;
  4. Invited to participate robot education special investigation chaired by mayor Xu Qin;
  5. Members of the society were invited by Carnegie Mellon University to participate in the 15-day special course of the summer school of robotics.
  6. Representatives of the society attended the CNC course of CNC machine tools (2 days) in Shenzhen University;
  7. Participate in the planning of Shenzhen robot education laboratory (2015-2020);
  8. and the City Education Foundation signed cooperation to build “Field robot Center Laboratory”, construction and opening;
  9. Invited Professor Kanter of Carnegie Mellon University and Professor Wang Huaquan of Shenzhen University to carry on the lecture dialogue robot.
